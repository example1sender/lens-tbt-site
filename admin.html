How it works

Two buttons, that's it.

visibility

Save preview

Stores your edits in this browser only. Perfect for checking how things look before going live. Nobody else sees these — open the site in this same browser to preview.

rocket_launch

Publish live

Makes your edits show for everyone, on every device — phones included. Saves to your site instantly. No files, no redeploy. Visitors see it on their next page load.

Adding photos

  • uploadUpload from your phone or computer — full quality, no size limit.
  • linkPaste a link to any image hosted online.

Photos are stored on your own site automatically (built-in hosting) — there's nothing to set up and no Cloudinary account required anymore.
